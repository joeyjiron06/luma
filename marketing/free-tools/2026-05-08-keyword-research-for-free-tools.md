## Scoring rubric

**Effort** — assumed build as a client-side static **Tool Page** (ffmpeg.wasm or similar):

| Score | Build profile |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Single-file audio format conversion (drag → convert → download) |
| 2 | Video container conversion or frame extraction |
| 3 | Multi-step workflow, informational + tool hybrid, or niche UX |
| 4 | Video compositing, mobile-optimized compression, or heavy transcoding |
| 5 | Speech-to-text / transcription (model size, accuracy, infra) |

**Relevancy** — how naturally the tool leads a **Creator-Editor** toward Luma's post-recording vocal cleanup:

| Score | Funnel fit |
|---:|---|
| 5 | Extracts or converts audio from creator recordings — direct "now make it sound pro" CTA |
| 4 | Power-user audio extraction path (same workflow, narrower audience) |
| 3 | Audio-adjacent (transcription, alternate audio formats) |
| 2 | Same audience, different pain (video format, compression) |
| 1 | Unrelated to vocal/audio cleanup workflow |

---

## Summary

Initial Ahrefs pass across format conversion, audio extraction, and mobile video editing queries. After scoring, the top opportunities cluster around **audio extraction and WAV conversion** — high relevancy to Luma, low build effort, and strong SV/KD ratios. High-volume **video format converters** (`mp4 to webm`, `mp4 to mov`) are easy wins for traffic but weaker Luma funnels.

**Top 3 by priority score:**

1. `mp4 to wav` — Priority 5
2. `mp4 to wav converter` — Priority 5
3. `mp3 to wav converter` — Priority 4

## Early signals

**Build first (Priority 4–5):**

- `mp4 to wav` — 16K SV, KD 2, easiest build, strongest Luma bridge
- `mp4 to wav converter` — same tool, variant keyword coverage
- `mp3 to wav converter` — 8.2K SV but KD 8; same ffmpeg stack, worth one Tool Page targeting both MP4 and MP3 → WAV
- `how to extract audio from video on iphone` — KD 0, high relevancy; consider a mobile-friendly extract-audio tool or guide + tool hybrid

**Traffic plays, weaker Luma funnel (Priority 3):**

- `mp4 to webm`, `mp4 to mov` — huge SV, trivial KD, but video-format pain ≠ vocal cleanup
- `convert mp3 to wav` and long-tail MP3 variants — high relevancy, KD 9–10 drags priority down

**Deprioritize for now (Priority 1–2):**

- `mp4 to png`, `mp4 to jpg` — image extraction, no Luma bridge
- iPhone music-overlay keywords — high effort, wrong pain point
- `mp4 to transcript` — meaningful SV but transcription is a 5/5 build effort
