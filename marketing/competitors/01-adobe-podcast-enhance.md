## User Research & Pain Points

**Source:** Reddit r/premiere — Adobe Podcast Enhance V2 launch thread
> "Since Adobe updated to V2 of their podcast enhancer, my latest enhanced audio files have included some bonus content: other people!"
- AI processing alters natural voice character
- No control over processing intensity in free tier
- Not integrated in Premiere Pro yet — web-only workflow friction
- **Ghost voices from other users bled into silence sections** (V2 privacy bug)
- Unpredictable output quality post-update
- Sentiment: **Negative**

**Source:** Adobe Community Forum — quality regression thread
> "Enhance Speech has somehow gotten worse."
- AI audio tool quality can regress with updates unexpectedly
- Lost sibilance/s-sounds make voices sound muffled and unnatural
- No rollback option when tool gets worse after update
- Sentiment: **Negative**

**Source:** Video producer torture test
- Great for fixing terrible audio; degrades good audio
- Fails on non-American accents/languages
- Cannot handle music — treats it as noise to remove
- Binary output — no control over enhancement level in free tier
- Sentiment: **Neutral**

**Source:** Professional podcast consultant review
> "It's ideal for remote guests with bad audio but not for your main host mic."
- Tool designed for damage control, not quality enhancement
- V2 voice alteration makes hosts sound like different people
- No way to apply selectively to only noisy segments
- Sentiment: **Neutral**

**Source:** Nearstream.us review
- Privacy concerns about uploading audio to third-party cloud
- Daily usage limits block professional/high-volume use
- Cloud dependency means no offline workflow
- No local Mac app — browser-only friction
- Sentiment: **Neutral**

**Source:** Comparison vs Descript Studio Sound (thepodcasthaven.com)
> "Adobe Speech Enhancement: voices sound digitized and unnatural with traffic noise."
- Heavy echo/reverb remains difficult for all AI tools
- Natural voice character preservation is the key differentiator users care about
- Sentiment: **Neutral**
