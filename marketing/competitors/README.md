## Key User Pain Points (cross-competitor)

Sourced from Reddit, review sites, and community forums:

- **Untreated room acoustics** is the #1 challenge for home creators
- **All AI de-reverb tools degrade audio quality** to some degree — users accept partial fixes
- **AI tools alter voice character** — robotic/muffled/digitized sound is the top complaint
- **Subscription pricing resented** — users strongly prefer one-time purchase
- **Cloud-only tools** raise privacy concerns (uploading audio to third-party servers)
- **CPU/GPU resource drain** causes recording failures (NVIDIA Broadcast, local plugins)
- **Intermittent noise** (traffic, footsteps, voices) unsolved by current AI tools
- **Tool quality regresses with updates** — no rollback options
- **No single tool handles all audio problems** — workflow fragmentation is painful
- **Learning curve of pro tools** (iZotope RX) deters beginners who discover them too late
