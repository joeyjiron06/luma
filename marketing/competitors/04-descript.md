## User Research & Pain Points

**Source:** Reddit r/podcasting — manual reverb removal attempt
> "Result sounds 'a little over processed.'"
- AI reverb removal creates over-processed, unnatural sound
- Descript Studio Sound at higher settings sounds robotic
- Manual post-correction of AI artifacts is time-consuming
- No easy way to control processing intensity
- Sentiment: **Negative**

**Source:** Comparison vs Adobe Podcast Enhance (thepodcasthaven.com)
> "Descript Studio Sound: more natural-sounding, better preserves voice character."
- Both tools have limits with heavy echo
- Natural voice character preservation is the key differentiator
- Descript wins for everyday use; Adobe wins only for extreme noise (with artifacts)
- Sentiment: **Neutral**
